Welcome!

The Guidance,Counseling and Career Development Directorate of Accra Technical University was establised to provide regular time and space for students to talk about their problems and explore difficult feelings in an environment that is dependable, confidential and free from distraction.

Objectives

The objectives of the Directorate are as follows:

    To encourage students' academic,social,emotional and personal development.In other words,it is aimed at helping a counselee adjust properly to family,school,peer association, and the society in general.

    To assist students to identify and recognise their interest,value,abilities and capacities,skills and positive personality traits and to match these with their chosen careers.

    To equip students with knowledge and prepare them for the world of work or self-employment.

    To organise training workshops and seminars on career and occupational information,study habits,personla traits etc. to address students' needs.
